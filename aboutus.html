HBX CORE

An Exclusive Joint Certificate Course by Harvard Business School offered with PGDM- Business Analytics and PGDM-BA at The ASM Group of Institutes, IIBR

 

The Programme

The HBX Credential of Readiness (CORe) is an online programme on business fundamentals developed exclusively by Harvard Business School since 2015. The programme is a digital learning initiative that leverages technology and multimedia to deliver an engaging online learning experience to business leaders around the world. With real-world, practical case studies and an interactive, social course platform, HBX is extending business education and the pursuit of excellence in teaching and learning into the digital age. Reinvent the future of education with HBX.

Learning Model of HBX CORe

HBX CORe courses are built to deliver an experience synonymous to the HBS classes on ampus. Every course is built on case study method of instruction to provide students with a holistic and insightful industry relevant learning experience that analyses situations like an industry expert. Every case-study in HBX CORe begins which a protagonist introduces the context and situation of the case. The later parts include a combination of texts, videos of faculty and case protagonist, animations and interactive learning exercises.

Interactive elements of the CORe learning experience include ‘cold calls’ during which students are selected at random to answer a question about the case. The case study also includes ungraded and graded quizzes with MCQs in each course module, optional weekly written assignments (CORe Connections), polls, interactive graphs, and a spreadsheet emulator.

Peer-to-peer learning is the heart of HBX CORe’s learning experience. Students take CORe along with 350-400 other students on the social platform. Each page of the course content includes points of access to various peer-to-peer students on the social platform. Each page of the course content includes points of access to various peer-to-peer learning tools. Students can pose queries, raise questions to specific course content on pages and answer questions that have been asked by others. Conversations are monitored by course content teams at HBX to ensure every questionis answered correctly, but the dialogue is driven by peers surfacing insights and applying course content to different contexts.

Learning Model of IBM

The Research and Business Analytics programme with IBM is paving the way for a new and highly specialized corporate role amongst students at the ASM Group of Institutes. The programme offers intensive training by the ASM faculty and industry experts.
The programme includes a host of statistic and dynamic sessions where each module will be concluded by industry sessions for the same.

Programme Delivery

The HBX CORe Programme is offered with multiple durations of study from 8-18 weeks. The entire course is delivered online with a set date of commencement because one undertakes the course along with a group of peers. The course is integrated and divided into modules with these set dates. Students must complete the course content and the quizzes at the end of the module at regular intervals. Students can log into the course platform, complete assignments, and interact with peers at any time of day. The program ends with a single 3 hour final examination. The goal of the programme is to offer learners a varied experience that balances the programmes schedule with their particular lifestyle and responsibilities.
